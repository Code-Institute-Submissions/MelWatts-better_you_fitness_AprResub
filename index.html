    Weights

    Weight lifting isn't just about bulking up and building muscle mass, the experts say. Its benefits include improved posture, better sleep, gaining bone density, maintaining weight loss, boosting metabolism, lowering inflammation and staving off chronic disease, among a laundry list of positives.

    Fitness

    Both weightlifting and cardio offer many of the same health benefits. They both reduce blood pressure and cholesterol levels, boost insulin sensitivity, and burn calories, which can help with weight loss. People also use fitness for mental health issues.

    Nutrition

    Weight lifting nutrition focuses on different things than nutrition for sports that demand endurance, such as long-distance running or speed skating. Instead, strength training nutrition focuses on enhancing protein intake to build stronger muscles. The amino acids found in protein are the building blocks of muscles.
